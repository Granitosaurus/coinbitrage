
$ coinbitrage BTC bitfinex gdax

---| Bitfinex | Gdax |
BTC| 100      | 150  |
LTC| 10       | 11   |

Supplied:
* coin type, exchange1, exchange2
Returns:
* pricing of supplied coin
* closest other coin pricing
Additional features:
* black/white list of coin matching, i.e. to avoid coins with slow and expensive transactions.
